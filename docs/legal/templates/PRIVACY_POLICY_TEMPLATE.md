Electric Sheep ("we", "our", or "us") is a personal, non-profit Android application for mood management. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Email Address**: Collected during account creation/authentication
- **Mood Entries**: Data you enter into the app (mood ratings, notes, timestamps)

### Technical Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data**: App usage patterns, feature usage
- **Error Logs**: Technical error information (for debugging)

### Data Storage
- **Local Storage**: Some data stored locally on your device
- **Cloud Storage**: Data synchronized with Supabase (backend service)

## How We Use Your Information

- **App Functionality**: To provide mood tracking and management features
- **Data Synchronization**: To sync data across your devices
- **Improvement**: To improve app functionality and user experience
- **Error Resolution**: To diagnose and fix technical issues

# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: January 20, 2025

## Introduction

Electric Sheep ("we", "our", or "us") is a personal, non-profit Android application for mood management. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Email Address**: Collected during account creation/authentication via Supabase
- **Mood Entries**: Data you enter into the app (mood ratings, notes, timestamps)
- **Authentication Data**: OAuth tokens and session information (managed by Supabase)

### Technical Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version (for app functionality)
- **Usage Data**: App usage patterns, feature usage (local only, not transmitted)
- **Error Logs**: Technical error information (local only, for debugging)

### Data Storage
- **Local Storage**: Some data stored locally on your device using Room database
- **Cloud Storage**: Data synchronized with Supabase (backend service)
  - Supabase Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy
  - Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest

## How We Use Your Information

- **App Functionality**: To provide mood tracking and management features
- **Data Synchronization**: To sync data across your devices
- **Authentication**: To secure your account and data
- **Improvement**: To improve app functionality and user experience (local analytics only)
- **Error Resolution**: To diagnose and fix technical issues (local error logs only)

## Data Sharing

**We do not sell your data.**

We may share data with:
- **Supabase**: Backend service provider (data storage and synchronization)
  - Supabase acts as a data processor under GDPR
  - Supabase Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy
- **No Third-Party Analytics**: We do not use third-party analytics services
- **No Advertising**: We do not share data with advertising networks

## Data Security

- Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS)
- Data is encrypted at rest (Supabase)
- Authentication required for data access
- Local data stored securely on device (Android app sandboxing)
- No sensitive data stored in plain text

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data (via app or email)
- **Export**: Export your data (feature may be added in future)
- **Correction**: Correct inaccurate data (via app)

To exercise these rights, contact: charliemic@users.noreply.github.com

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Local Data**: Stored on device until app is uninstalled or data is cleared

## Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
